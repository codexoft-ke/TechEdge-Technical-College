Get in Touch with TechEdge

We welcome your inquiries and look forward to helping you take the next step in your educational and career journey. Whether you're interested in learning more about our programs, scheduling a campus visit, or discussing how TechEdge Technical College can help you achieve your goals, our admissions and student services teams are here to provide you with personalized assistance and guidance.

Our campus is conveniently located in the heart of the technology corridor, easily accessible by public transportation and with ample parking for those who prefer to drive. We encourage prospective students and their families to visit our campus to experience firsthand our state-of-the-art facilities, meet our faculty, and get a feel for the dynamic learning environment that makes TechEdge special.

Campus Visit and Information Sessions

We strongly encourage prospective students to visit our campus and attend one of our regular information sessions. These sessions provide an excellent opportunity to learn about our programs, meet faculty and current students, tour our facilities, and get answers to your questions about admissions, financial aid, and career outcomes. Information sessions are held every Tuesday and Thursday at 6:00 PM, and Saturday mornings at 10:00 AM. No appointment is necessary, but we appreciate advance notice if you plan to attend.

For those who cannot attend our regular information sessions, we are happy to arrange individual campus tours and meetings with admissions counselors. Simply call our admissions office or use the contact form above to schedule your visit. We can also arrange meetings with program directors and faculty members if you have specific questions about particular academic programs or career paths.
