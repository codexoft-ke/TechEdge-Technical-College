Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

When do classes start?

We offer three start dates per year: Fall (September), Spring (January), and Summer (May). Rolling admissions are available for most programs.

Do you offer online programs?

Yes! Most of our programs offer hybrid options with both online and in-person components. Some programs are available fully online.

What are the admission requirements?

High school diploma or equivalent, basic computer literacy, and motivation to succeed. No programming experience required for most programs.

How long are the programs?

Program lengths vary from 15-24 months depending on the specialization. Most core programs are 18-20 months.

Do you help with job placement?

Absolutely! We have a 95% job placement rate and offer comprehensive career services including resume help, interview prep, and job matching.

Can I transfer credits?

Yes, we accept transfer credits from accredited institutions. Our admissions team will evaluate your transcripts for applicable credits.
