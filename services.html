Admission Requirements

Simple, straightforward requirements to get you started

General Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Basic computer literacy
  • Strong motivation to learn and succeed
  • Completed application form
  • Personal statement (500 words)
  • Two references (academic or professional)

Recommended Preparation:

  • Basic mathematics (algebra recommended)
  • Introduction to programming (helpful but not required)
  • Problem-solving experience

Application Process

  1. Submit Application: Complete our online application form
  2. Document Review: We review your transcripts and references
  3. Interview: Brief interview with admissions counselor
  4. Assessment: Optional skills assessment for program placement
  5. Financial Planning: Meet with financial aid counselor
  6. Enrollment: Secure your spot and begin your journey

Application Deadlines:

Fall Semester: July 15
Spring Semester: December 15
Summer Semester: April 15
